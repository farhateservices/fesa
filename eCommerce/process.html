Ordering Procedure

Online Ordering Procedure

  1. Add the items with quanity in the card using 'Add to Cart' button avaialable below each item.

  2. Look into the cart by clicking on the 'My Cart' button available at top right corner of the page also at end of the page and confirm you order details.

  3. Place your order using either 'Order via WhatsApp' or 'Order via Email' button.

  4. Once you click on either of the buttons to place order it will open up your Gmail/WhatsaApp with all order details and Total Amount, you just have to press the send button in your app

  5. For online orders we require pre-payment so kindly make the payment of Total Amount as in the order details to our UPI Id: anyid@upi or the QR code available below in this page.

  6. Please send us the payment refernces/screenshot to ease out the process along with Your Address details where the vegetables needs to be delivered (if opting for home delivery).

  7. We will confirmn the realization of payment and also intimate when your order is packed and ready to pick up/deliver

Please note that # in the item name indicates that those perticular items are sold @ per piece but cart & order details might show 'piece' as 'kg' but it will mean 'piece' only.

Please do not make any kind of changes in the 'Order Details' or 'Total' amount manually, add/remove the items according to your preference in the cart only and same will be reflected in the order details. Also do not refresh the page while placing an order or adding items in the cart.
