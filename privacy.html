Privacy Notice

Welcome to the Privacy Notice of Farhat e Services & Assistance. We prioritize your privacy and want to assure you of how we collect, use, and protect your data:

Information Collection

We highly value your privacy and do not extensively collect personal data or use cookies on our website. If you choose to engage with us through our "Send a Message" feature on the Contacts page, we will request your name, email, and a message on a voluntary basis. This information is solely intended for communication purposes related to inquiries, feedback, or other requests. We want to assure you that our updated communication channels are designed with the utmost security to protect your data.

Data Usage

Your "Send a Message" data is exclusively utilized to facilitate communication with you. We do not share this information with any third parties. Your trust is of utmost importance to us, and we handle your data with the highest level of care and respect.

Data Security

While we implement robust security measures, it's important to acknowledge that no internet transmission is entirely secure. By using our messaging feature, you acknowledge and accept the inherent risks associated with online communication.

Data Retention

We retain your communication data for as long as necessary. Following your initial inquiry or service, we may use your information to send relevant updates or promotions. You have the freedom to opt out of our emails at any time. If you wish to have your data deleted, please inform us. This applies to messages across various platforms such as WhatsApp, Instagram, and more. Your choices and privacy are paramount.

Your Rights and Feedback

You possess the right to access, update, or rectify the information you provide. If you wish to exercise these rights or have your data removed from our records, please utilize the contact information provided in the "Contact Us" section. Your feedback is invaluable to us, and we encourage you to get in touch with any concerns or suggestions to enhance our services.

Changes to This Notice

This Privacy Notice may be periodically updated without prior notice. We recommend revisiting this page to stay informed about changes. Your continued use of our website after such changes signifies your acknowledgment of them.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or feedback about our Privacy Notice, please reach out to us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section. We are here to assist you and ensure the protection of your privacy.
