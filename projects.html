Our Web Projects

  • E-Commerce Website

    • Embark on an exciting journey with our E-Commerce platform—a fusion of convenience and innovation. Seamlessly integrating WhatsApp and Gmail, this platform revolutionizes order communication, while automated bill calculation ensures pricing accuracy without the hassle. Engage with captivating product displays and transparent pricing, designed to create an immersive shopping experience tailored for Indian small businesses. With user-friendliness as our compass, the platform offers effortless UPI payment processing, simplifying transactions. Guided by a philosophy of simplicity and professionalism, enjoy curated products and an intuitive cart section as you navigate. Explore essential policy pages, including ordering process, contact us, privacy policy, refund policy, and about us, all contributing to a holistic customer journey. Within our portfolio, this project shines as a testament to our unwavering commitment to delivering excellence in web development, perfectly attuned to the needs of Indian small businesses.

  • Portfolio/Business Website

    • Discover my Portfolio/Business Website – a platform that showcases my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Designed with mobile users in mind, this website offers a smooth and intuitive experience on your smartphone. Swipe through my projects, delve into my achievements, and explore my capabilities – all within a responsive and user-friendly interface. From concise project descriptions to dynamic interactions, my portfolio comes to life on your mobile device. Immerse yourself in the world of web development and innovation, conveniently accessible at your fingertips.

  • Amazon Clone (Best viewed on laptops/pcs)

    • Experience the Amazon Clone Website project – a meticulous recreation of the Amazon.com international version using only CSS and HTML. Our skilled developers and designers have crafted this clone with precision, emulating the look and feel of Amazon's renowned user interface. Explore a range of products, add them to your cart, and simulate the checkout process – all while enjoying the familiarity of Amazon's layout. This project is optimized for laptops and PCs, providing a seamless and authentic browsing experience similar to the original platform.
